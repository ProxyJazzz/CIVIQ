## Executive Summary

**CIVIQ** is an AI-powered infrastructure reporting platform that transforms how communities report, validate, and solve local infrastructure problems. Using Google Gemini for intelligent image analysis and real-time validation through community engagement, we turn scattered complaints into actionable data that cities can't ignore.

**Problem:** Infrastructure issues are invisible, fragmented, and never resolved.
**Solution:** AI + community = visible, validated, solvable problems.
**Differentiator:** Gemini Vision AI automatically categorizes and prioritizes issues with 94%+ accuracy.

## The Pitch (90 seconds)

### Hook

"Imagine if every pothole, water leak, or broken streetlight could **talk**—telling you exactly what it is, how urgent it is, and how many people are affected. That's CIVIQ."

### Problem (20 seconds)

"Today, infrastructure reporting is **broken**. A resident spots a pothole. They call 311. They wait. Nothing happens. They're frustrated. The city has no data on priorities. And the problem stays there—sometimes for years.

**Result:** Preventable accidents. Billions in reactive maintenance. And residents who feel powerless."

### Solution (20 seconds)

"CIVIQ changes that. Take a photo of an infrastructure problem. Our AI instantly recognizes it, categorizes it, and assesses severity. Your report goes live to the community.

Neighbors see it. They upvote to validate. **Instantly**, local leaders and city officials see what problems matter most, where, and why."

### Why AI? (15 seconds)

"Google Gemini Vision is game-changing here. It's not just accurate—it's fast. A photo becomes actionable data in seconds. And it works at scale. Whether it's 100 reports or 100,000, the AI handles it."

### Why Different? (10 seconds)

"Citizens report infrastructure issues to fragmented channels. Facebook, 311, Twitter. No coordination. No data.

CIVIQ is **the unified platform** where problems become visible, prioritized, and—crucially—solved."

**That's the power of transparency plus AI.**"

Help us fix your infrastructure."
